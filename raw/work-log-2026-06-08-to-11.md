## 4. Topic Affinity System

EMA-based per-user per-topic scoring หลัง quiz completion

### Migration 0016 (`workspace/aunjai-swarm/middleware/migrations/0016_topic_affinity_table.sql`):
- ตาราง `topic_affinity`:
  - `user_id + topic_tag` (UNIQUE)
  - `affinity_score` — 0-100, higher = stronger knowledge in topic
  - `weakness_score` — 0-100, higher = weaker area
  - `total_responses`, `correct_count`, `incorrect_count`

### Service (`workspace/aunjai-swarm/middleware/services/topic_affinity_service.py`):
- `upsert_topic_affinity(db, user_id, topic_tag, score)` — EMA upsert, α=0.2
- Formula: `affinity = old × 0.8 + (score × 100) × 0.2`; `weakness = old × 0.8 + ((1-score) × 100) × 0.2`
- Called from `process_quiz_complete()` ใน line_webhook.py — additive, fail-safe (ไม่ crash ถ้า upsert ล้มเหลว)

### Academy Model v3 (`workspace/aunjai-swarm/middleware/models/academy_v3.py`):
- เพิ่ม `topic_tags TEXT[]` ใน CurriculumSet
- Status enum: DRAFT / ACTIVE / LOCKED
- Certificate status: 9 states ตั้งแต่ PENDING_FORM ถึง DELIVERED/FAILED_DELIVERY
